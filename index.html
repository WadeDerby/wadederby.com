About Me

I am currently a Web Developer at Krecent Technologies, a company I co-founded in 2016, where I work with a team of talented engineers to design and develop innovative technology driven solutions.

My current stack is Vue.js on the frontend and Laravel on the backend. I have also undertaken some projects using ASP.NET, Node.js and Python.

My journey began at age of 16 in High School where I taught myself programming in Pascal. I recently obtained a Bachelor of Science Degree in Computer Science and Engineering. During the same period as my studies I worked as a freelancer on graphic design and web development projects. Some of the projects I have worked on include a chat app and an online voting system.

In my spare time I can be found watching a basketball game, listening to music or mentoring other web developers.
