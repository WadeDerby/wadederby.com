About Me

I am a Web Developer at Krecent Technologies, a company I co-founded in 2016, where I work together with a team of talented engineers to design and develop innovative technology driven solutions.

My current stack is Vue.js on the frontend and Laravel on the backend. I have also undertaken some projects using ASP.NET, Node.js and Python.

My journey began in High School where I taught myself programming in Pascal at the age of sixteen. Now, I have a Bachelor of Science Degree in Computer Science and Engineering. While I was a student, I worked part-time as a freelancer on graphic design and web development projects. Some of the projects I have worked on include a chat app and an online voting system.

In my spare time I love watching basketball games and listening to music. I'm also very keen on mentoring other web developers because I believe in the potential of technology to create to a better world and I would like to contribute my bit to seeing that happen.
